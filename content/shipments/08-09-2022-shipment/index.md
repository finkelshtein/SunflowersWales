A vast shipment of medical supplies, including 50 pairs of Israeli bandages (£850),100 hemostatic dressing folds (£1450), and a drone (£1730), was sent to Ukraine.

<!--more-->

We have also sent ointments, vitamins, other medicine, and glucose meters.

The parcels were sent to Ukrainian volunteers in Chernomorsk and Uzhhorod. (See FB-posts:
